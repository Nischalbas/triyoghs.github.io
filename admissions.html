Admission

1. Elegibility
1.1 - For Kindergarten(KG), Students should be atleast 5 years old. Enrollment is on first-come-first-basis if other criteria are met
1.2 - For Class I and above, should there be any vacancy, the child must have completed the earlier Grade.

2. Application Form
2.1 - Application Forms for admission are available at the School Office and online at www.triyog.edu.np.
2.2 - Applicants should submit these forms duly filled in with all the supporting documents by the given deadline.
3. Admission Test
3.1 - Getting through the Test - written and oral is compulsory. This screening appropriately assesses a child's language skills - English B Nepali, numeric comprehension - Mathematics and other abilities to determine his/her/their school readiness
4. Parents Interview
4.1 - The School invariably conducts an interview of the parents of the applicant.
4.1 - The School invariably conducts an interview of the parents of the applicant.
4.2 - The Interview is held on the same day as the child's Admission Test.
5. Results
5.1 - The results are strictly based on the order of merit and normally declared the very next day of the Admission Test and Parents' Interview.
6. Enrollment
6.1 - Successful applicants shall be enrolled as bona fide Triyog student on completion of this process alongside the payment of admission fees to the School's bank account.
